The total fertitlity rate in the world

The total fertility rate is the number of children that would be born to a woman if she would live to the end of her childbearing years. Many people are worried the world population will continue to grow to unsustainable numbers. However, this visualisation shows that the fertility rate appears to converge to around 2 births per women. If the current trend continues the world population will stabilize.

This visualisation shows the fertility rate for all countries from 1960 untill 2013. Clicking on a country will show the history of the fertility rate in the country. Clicking on a year in the linegraph will show the fertility rate for the world in that year.
